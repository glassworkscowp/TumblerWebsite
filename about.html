Our Story

Glassworks Co is a student-owned business located in Williams Parkway, founded by a group of passionate entrepreneurs dedicated to creating sustainable products that make a positive impact on our planet.

Our journey began with a simple idea: why not create beautiful, functional products that also give back to the community? Every glass tumbler we craft uses recycled materials and bamboo lids, ensuring minimal environmental footprint while providing you with premium quality.

What sets us apart is our commitment to charity. 100% of our profits are donated to various environmental and community initiatives, making every purchase a step towards a better world.
