About Us

Glassworks Co is a student-owned business located in Williams Parkway. We are a group of aspiring entrepreneurs dedicated to creating sustainable products that make a positive impact.

Our glass tumblers with bamboo lids and awesome elegant designs are crafted with eco-friendly materials, ensuring minimal environmental footprint. Every purchase supports charitable causes, as 100% of our profits are donated to various charities.

join us in our mission to promote sustainability and give back to the community, one tumbler at a time!
